The plot shows two different displays of density. The displays might be from the same distribution or two different distributions.

( a ) What are the two displays?

ANSWER: The rug plot is the small ticks on the horizontal axis. The other distribution has a shape that increases steadily — never decreasing — starting from 0 on the left and going to 1 on the right. That's the shape of a cumulative distribution.
