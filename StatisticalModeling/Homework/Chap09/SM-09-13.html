Intro to Statistical Modeling Ch. 9 Prob. 13

Consider two models, Model 1 and Model 2, with the same response variable.

  • Model 1 is nested in Model 2 if the of Model 1 are a subset of those of Model 2.

    • If Model 1 is nested in Model 2, then model 1 cannot have a higher \( R^2 \) than model 2.

  • Which of the following are nested in A ~ B \( \star \) C + D?

    • A ~ B
    • A ~ B + D
    • B ~ C
    • A ~ B + C + D
    • A ~ B \( \star \) D + C
    • A ~ D
