Intro to Statistical Modeling Ch. 2 Prob. 2.22

Since the computer has to represent numbers that are both very large and very small, scientific notation is often used. The number 7.23e4 means \( 7.23\times 10^4 = 72300 \). The number 1.37e-2 means \( 1.37\times 10^{-2} = 0.0137 \).

For each of the following numbers written in computer scientific notation, say what is the corresponding number.

  • 3e1

  • 1e3

  • 0.1e3

  • 0.3e-2

  • 10e3

  • 10e-3

  • 0.0003e3
