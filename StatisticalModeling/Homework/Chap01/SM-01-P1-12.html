Intro to Statistical Modeling Ch.1 Prob. 1.12

The following statement gives a result that some people are surprised by:

>10e3 == 1000
[1] FALSE

Explain why the result was FALSE rather than TRUE.

ANSWER: The computer returns FALSE because the quantities being compared with == are not equal. 10e3 is ten-thousand in scientific notation. It's 1e3 that is one-thousand.
